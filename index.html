The methodology

Self-Derived Knowledge

Most tutors teach students to recognize problem types and recall procedures. This can make it difficult for your student to implement the knowledge in different situations.

In my sessions, we work from physical intuition and mathematical logic, so students can reconstruct any formula they need and solve problems they've never seen before.
