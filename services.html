Business Policies

Clear expectations,
for everyone.

These policies protect your time and mine. Please read them before booking — they apply without exception.

Policy 01

24-Hour Cancellation

Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice forfeit the session in full. It is billed at 100% of the hourly rate. Reschedules follow the same rule.

Policy 02

No-Show Policy

If a student does not appear within 15 minutes of the scheduled start — whether at the library or on Zoom — the session is considered forfeited and billed in full.

Policy 03

Academic Integrity

I will guide your student through logic and teach problem-solving strategies. I will never complete graded homework, take-home quizzes, or exams on a student's behalf. No exceptions.
